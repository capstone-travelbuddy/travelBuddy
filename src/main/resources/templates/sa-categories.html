Welcome to San Antonio!

Help our city stand out! Local businesses add to the character and success of the community you’re visiting. Not only is it an economic advantage for the town, but you foster a happy and unique experience for you, your friends and family! In comparison to larger chain stores, local businesses generally recycle money back into the local economy. Likewise, when visitors shop at local businesses those dollars stay within the local economy. All of these aspects help with the economic development within our city.

Local Coffee Shops

Coffee is booming in San Antonio. While the family at What’s Brewing? Coffee Roasters has been roasting in the Alamo City since 1981, it’s only been in recent years that locals have had their choice of high-quality roasts from dozens of local roasters and cafes.

Local Breweries

San Antonio’s landscape is a mecca for micro-brew fans and is only getting better. For those looking at what the city has to offer, taking a weekend to map out a craft beer jaunt from the Blue Star Arts Complex to Pearl Brewery, provides a number of amazing craft experiences.
